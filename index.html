About Us

Hobart Milk Factory processes and delivers local milk 6 days/week. It is operated by brothers Ben and James Green.

Our Story

Hobart Milk is a family owned and operated local milk processing business. We use the best local milk from a single farm in the beautiful coastal Bream Creek Cove overlooking Marion Bay. We deliver to your business in the Hobart area 6 days per week.

Who We Help

We pay the dairy farmer almost double the price that they have been receiving from the large distributers allowing the farmer to grow the business.
Our Full Cream milk is a high-quality unstandardised milk meaning it is bottled as it came from the farm. This creates an exceptional product for drinking and for baristas to use and satisfy the most discerning coffee customer; a milk with body, taste and texture.
Our milk is processed and bottled within hours from collection and is ready to be delivered to you the same day. Contact us today to support your local dairy.
